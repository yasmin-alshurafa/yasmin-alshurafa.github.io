- Connecting From Distance -

............


This 30 Minute Film Festival entry titled "Connecting From Distance" tells the story of how three students from different parts of the world studying at New York University in Abu Dhabi connect with their family and friends in this digital world. The film depicts the use of different languages and messaging apps by the students depending on where the student is coming from.

Connecting and disconnecting through the use of technology is the underlying theme of this film.

Note: This film was filmed and edited in 30 minutes.
