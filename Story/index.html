-Cinderella's Story-

Once upon a time, there was a beautiful girl named Cinderella. Cinderella lived with her stepmother and stepsisters.

Cinderella's stepmother didn't love her and made her do all the household work alone. While she did the work, her stepsisters would make fun of her cleaning.

One day, they received a letter that the king was having a ball and everyone was invited. Everyone got excited because the prince was going to choose a wife from the ball.

Cinderella wanted to go to the ball but her stepmother left her back in the home. Cinderella was sad and went outside to the garden where she saw her fairy godmother.

The godmother helped Cinderella to go to the ball. she gave her a new gown, glass heels, and a black horse to drop her at the ball. However, before Cinderalla left for the ball, her godmother told her that the magic will only last until midnight.

Cinderella reached the ball and everyone was looking at her beauty. She was the prettiest girl at the ball that even her stepsisters didn't recognize her. She danced all night with the prince. He fell in love with her and all the girls were jealous from her.

She remembered that the magic will go at midnight, so she left the ball and ran outside. One of her heels fell when she was running back.

The prince took her heel and because he fell in love with Cinderella, he started looking around every house for the girl whose foot fits in the glass heel.

When the prince reached Cinderella's house, her stepsisters tried to fit their feet into the glass, but they couldn't make it. Finally, Cinderella tried it and it fit her perfectly.

The prince recognized her from the ball night. Cinderella married the prince and they lived happily ever after.
